Privacy Policy

This mini app is designed to operate fully non‑custodially inside World App. We do not collect personal data beyond what is required to render the UI and interact with World App MiniKit.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • No private keys or seed phrases.
  • No biometric data or sensitive identifiers.
  • No off‑chain balances or custody.

Data We Use

  • Wallet address and username provided by World App via MiniKit for display and on‑chain actions.
  • On‑chain data (balances, events) via a public RPC provider to show vault state.

World ID Verification

If enabled, verification happens in World App. We only consume the result needed to gate features; we do not store verification data.
